ReShot

Shoot your bullets through time!

A 2D platformer shooter, but with a twist: You’ve got a Time-Loop Gun.

Overview

ReShot is a fast-paced 2D platformer shooter, but with a twist: You’ve got a Time-Loop Gun. Fire a bullet. Die. Respawn. That same bullet now travels through time, appearing in your next life exactly when and where it was first shot..

Plan your shots. Die with purpose. Use your past selves' bullets to open paths, defeat enemies, and outsmart the level itself.

My Role

  • Developing the visual side or the project
  • UX and UI Design
  • Designed core gameplay systems
  • Built narrative branching structure
  • Implemented decision and consequence logic
  • Handled audio direction and atmosphere

Game and Level Design

Here's my work in the field of game and level design.

Starting Point

Base environment without lighting or post processing. Focused on layout clarity and diegetic object placement.

Lighting Pass

High contrast lighting with aggressive highlights to push mood. Darkness used as a design tool, not just atmosphere.

Post Processing

Custom LUT system with 20+ palettes. Full stack tuning including bloom, contrast curves, and tonal compression.

Art

I created some of the art assets for the game. I focused on making a set of bits that could be assembled together in various ways.

Outcome

Delivered a fully playable vertical slice within two months. The project demonstrated strong narrative cohesion, atmospheric direction, and systems clarity.
